# Maintainers

Mnema is built and maintained by **BOPPL** (The Boring People). The honest
picture of who steers it:

## Lead maintainer (BDFL)

- **Nischay** — [@nbkdoesntknowcoding](https://github.com/nbkdoesntknowcoding)

Nischay is the lead maintainer and the project's benevolent dictator for life:
the final call on direction, what lands, and what ships rests here. See
[GOVERNANCE.md](./GOVERNANCE.md) for how decisions get made.

## Contributors

Mnema is better for the people who file issues, report bugs, and send pull
requests. Contributions arrive through the fork → PR flow in
[CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md); accepted changes are credited to their
authors (authorship and `Co-authored-by` preserved) when they ship. Thank you to
everyone who's pitched in.

If you'd like to take on a larger, ongoing role in the project, open an issue or
reach out — maintainership is earned through sustained, trusted contribution, at
the lead maintainer's discretion.

## Reaching the maintainers

- **General / support / bugs / features** — [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/nbkdoesntknowcoding/mnema/issues)
  (see [SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md)).
- **Security** — privately, per [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
- **Conduct** — mnema@theboringpeople.in (see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)).
- **Enterprise / commercial** — via the website.
